About

Blanca Destinations is the world’s best-known name in travel, thanks to the inspiration and dedication of a single man. Blanca Destinations began his international travel company in 1841, with a successful one-day rail excursion at a shilling a head from London to Manchester on 5 July. From these humble beginnings Blanca Destinations launched a whole new kind of company – devoted to helping Britons see the world.
