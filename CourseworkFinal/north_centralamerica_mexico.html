Iberotel Luxor Hotel

Description: Embracing a romantic fountain courtyard, luxurious Four Seasons Hotel Mexico, D.F., occupies a refined oasis in the heart of Mexico’s capital – just steps from Chapultepec Park and the exclusive Polanco neighbourhood.

Four Seasons Hotel México, D.F., offers 240 rooms, including 40 luxury suites, surrounding a beautifully landscaped fountain courtyard near Chapultepec Park and the exclusive Polanco district in Mexico City. With views across the garden or of city avenues, accommodations are both spacious and elegantly appointed.

Standard room features include LCD flat-screen televisions and wireless high-speed Internet access. Full marble bathrooms are designed with a deep soaking tub and a separate shower, as well as a WC in a private alcove

Enjoy relaxing spa treatments, heated outdoor pool, and luxurious fitness centre during your stay.
